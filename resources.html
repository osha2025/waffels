Frequently Asked Questions

Web accessibility means that websites, tools, and technologies are designed and developed so that people with disabilities can use them.

While primarily helping people with disabilities, accessibility features often improve the experience for all users, including mobile users and the elderly.

In many countries, including the US (ADA) and EU, accessibility is legally required for many types of websites.
